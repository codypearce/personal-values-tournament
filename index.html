Value Tournament

How it works:

You'll be presented with pairs of values and asked to choose which is more important to you.

Through a series of rounds, we'll narrow down from 100 values to your top 8-10 most important values.

In each round, you can select one value, the other, or both if they're equally important.

You'll have a limited number of "both" selections per round (10% of the values).
